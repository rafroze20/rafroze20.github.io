The Classic Pancake Recipe

What is a pancake?

A pancake (or hotcake, griddlecake, or flapjack, not to be confused with oat bar flapjacks) is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

Why is pancake one of my favourites?

I love it because it is tasty, easy to make and the consumption of time is really less than any other dishes I make. And I absolutely love it's eggy and milky aroma all over my kitchen!

INGREDIENTS:
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted

DIRECTIONS:

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.

    2. 🥣

  2. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

    3. 🧈

  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.

    4. 🔥

  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

    5. 🥄

  5. When brown on both sides, serve hot.

    6. 🍽

  6. Add sugar sprinkles, chocolate/ chocolate syrup, honey or fresh fruits to enhance taste.

    🍯

Essential tools for this recipe

  • Large bowl
  • Griddle
  • Frying pan
